# InterviewIQ AI - Project Brief & PRD

## 1. Project Overview
**InterviewIQ AI** is a premium, AI-native career preparation platform designed to help university students and software engineers master technical and behavioral interviews. By combining ATS resume analysis with immersive, real-time mock interview simulations, the platform provides actionable, data-driven feedback to bridge the gap between preparation and performance.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **University Students**: Seeking internships and entry-level roles.
*   **Software Engineers**: Preparing for technical rounds at top-tier tech companies.
*   **Career Switchers**: Looking to refine their interview presence and domain knowledge.

## 3. Design Philosophy
The visual identity is inspired by modern, high-productivity tools like **Linear, Notion, and ChatGPT**.
*   **Aesthetic**: Modern, Premium, and Futuristic.
*   **UI Patterns**: Glassmorphism, soft shadows, rounded cards (8px-12px), and generous whitespace.
*   **Color Palette**: 
    *   Primary: Professional Blue (#2563eb)
    *   Accents: Indigo and Purple for AI-driven features.
    *   Feedback: Success Green, Warning Orange, and Error Red.
*   **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter) with a focus on hierarchy and legibility.

## 4. Core Features & User Journey

### 4.1. Authentication
*   Professional split-screen Login/Signup.
*   Social auth integration (Continue with Google).
*   AI-themed brand illustrations to set the tone.

### 4.2. Central Dashboard
*   **Interview Readiness Score**: A high-level metric (0-100%) tracking overall preparation.
*   **Progress Tracking**: Weekly performance charts and skill improvement graphs.
*   **Quick Actions**: One-click access to Resume Analysis and Mock Interviews.

### 4.3. AI Resume Analyzer
*   **ATS Scoring**: Instant feedback on resume compatibility with applicant tracking systems.
*   **Keyword Analysis**: Identifying matched and missing keywords based on job descriptions.
*   **Improvement Suggestions**: Actionable advice on strengths and weaknesses.

### 4.4. Interview Arena
*   **Immersive Environment**: Clean, distraction-free interface for mock sessions.
*   **Real-time AI Feedback**: Center microphone with pulse animations and audio waveform visualizers.
*   **Progressive Difficulty**: Question navigator and real-time performance metrics (Technical vs. Behavioral).

### 4.5. AI Analysis & History (Planned)
*   Detailed post-interview reports with transcriptions.
*   STAR method evaluation and depth of knowledge scoring.
*   Historical performance tracking to visualize growth over time.

## 5. Technical Requirements
*   **Responsive Design**: Desktop-first with seamless mobile compatibility.
*   **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and clear focus states.
*   **Performance**: Lightweight animations and fast-loading skeleton states.
